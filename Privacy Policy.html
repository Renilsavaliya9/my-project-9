Privacy Policy - GujaratVibe.com

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through our website and does not extend to any data collected offline or via other platforms.

By using GujaratVibe.com, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our site, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Voluntary Information: Name, email, phone number, and message content when contacting us directly.

We use collected data for:
